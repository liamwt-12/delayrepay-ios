Questions
Is this Trainline?+
No. We're an independent service. Trainline sells tickets — we monitor your commute and catch delays you'd otherwise miss.
Do you submit claims for me?+
Free prepares everything — you submit it (takes 2 minutes). Pro tracks claims through to payment, so nothing slips through the cracks.
Does it work with my operator?+
Yes. All UK train operators that support Delay Repay — Avanti, LNER, GWR, SWR, Northern, and more.
Do you need my ticket?+
Not to monitor. We just need to know your route and times. You'll need your ticket details when you submit the claim.
How often do you check?+
Every 5 minutes during your commute window — morning and evening. We check even when the app is closed, and notify you if your train is delayed.
What does Pro do?+
Tracks deadlines, monitors claim status, shows your history, and reminds you to follow up. Free finds the money — Pro makes sure you get it.
